# Bug Report-004: Keyboard input delayed in the “Create Post” text field

**App:** Threads  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Environment:** iOS 18.6.2, iPhone XS  
**App Version:** Threads 327.0 (TestFlight)

---

## ✅ Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Threads  
2. Tap “Create Post”  
3. Start typing a long sentence (10+ words)

---

## ✅ Expected Result:
Keyboard input responds instantly.

---

## ✅ Actual Result:
Typing becomes noticeably delayed after 3–5 seconds.  
Characters appear with a 0.3–0.5s lag.

---

## ✅ Additional Notes:
- More noticeable when switching keyboard languages  
- Issue disappears after restarting the app  
- Likely related to text rendering performance
