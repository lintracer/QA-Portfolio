# Bug Report-002: Images fail to load in the feed (placeholder displayed)

**App:** Threads  
**Severity:** Medium  
**Priority:** High  
**Environment:** iOS 18.6.2, iPhone XS  
**App Version:** Threads 327.0 (TestFlight)

---

## ✅ Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the Threads app  
2. Scroll the feed slowly  
3. Observe posts that contain images

---

## ✅ Expected Result:
All images load normally without delay.

---

## ✅ Actual Result:
Some images do not load at all.  
A grey placeholder is shown instead of the image.

---

## ✅ Additional Notes:
- Occurs randomly on both Wi-Fi and mobile data  
- Restarting the app does not solve the issue  
- Happens in ~10–15% of posts
